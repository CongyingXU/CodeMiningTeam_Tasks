Unless that is impossible,<br/>\nin which case it splits it along the DOLLAR.</p>\n\n\n<p>Unfortunately, LoggerContext.getLogger() may then behave surprisingly</p>\n\n\n<p>The name \"A.B.C$D\" </p>\n\n<p>will give the Logger chain  A -&gt; A.B -&gt; A.B.C -&gt; A.B.C.D  as expected</p>\n\n\n<p>The name \"A.B$C.D\"</p>\n\n<p>could give the Logger chain  A -&gt; A.B -&gt; A.B.C -&gt; A.B.C.D but construction will<br/>\nfail!
